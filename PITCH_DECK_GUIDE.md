## üé§ Presentation Tips

### Before the Pitch
1. **Practice, practice, practice** - Know your content cold
2. **Time yourself** - Stay within time limits
3. **Anticipate questions** - Review the Q&A section
4. **Test technology** - Ensure slides work, links open, videos play
5. **Prepare backup** - Have PDF version ready

### During the Pitch
1. **Start strong** - Hook them with the problem
2. **Tell stories** - Use customer anecdotes
3. **Show passion** - Your enthusiasm is contagious
4. **Maintain eye contact** - Don't read slides
5. **Engage audience** - Ask rhetorical questions
6. **Handle interruptions gracefully** - Welcome questions
7. **Watch time** - Keep track of your pacing

### After the Pitch
1. **Send thank you** - Email within 24 hours
2. **Share materials** - Send executive summary PDF
3. **Follow up** - Schedule next steps
4. **Gather feedback** - Ask what resonated
5. **Iterate** - Improve based on feedback
