## üí° Pro Tips

### For Maximum Impact

**1. Storytelling**
Don't just present facts - tell the story:
- Start with a relatable problem (teacher wasting time)
- Show your "aha!" moment
- Describe the transformation you enable

**2. Show, Don't Tell**
- Include product demo in presentation
- Show actual dashboard screenshots
- Use customer video testimonials
- Display real attendance data

**3. Address Objections Early**
- Acknowledge competition exists
- Explain why you'll win
- Show customer validation
- Prove market demand

**4. Practice Your Delivery**
- Rehearse 10+ times before important meeting
- Record yourself and watch
- Get feedback from mentors
- Time every section

**5. Prepare for Questions**
Common questions included in QUICK_PITCH_GUIDE.md:
- Why will customers switch?
- What's your moat?
- How do you acquire customers?
- What if big players copy you?
